

*** Hello! Here's how to install your mailing list subscription form ***


1: Open the file NLprocess.php with a Text Editor (i.e. Notepad.exe) 

2: substitute the test email with your real newsletter email 
(It's the same used in SENDBLASTER Manage Subscription section).

3: Open the file newsletter.htm and copy the STYLE and the FORM lines to your personal webpage following the inside instructions.

4: Upload to your website the file NLprocess.php and your modified html page.




*** How To Manage more subscription lists ***

1 - Duplicate the entire "newsletterphp" directory
2 - Modify the "NLprocess.php" file with the email address of the second list.
3 - Cut and Paste the HTML code and styles from the file "newsletter.htm" to your website files.
4 - Upload in your website the files:

	* NLprocess.php
	* your modified webpage.htm
